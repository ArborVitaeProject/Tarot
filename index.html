Tarot Cards and Astrological Associations

Major Arcana

  • The Fool Uranus (modern astrology) / Air element (traditional astrology)

    Astrological Association: Uranus (modern astrology) / Air element (traditional astrology)

    Keywords: Beginnings, spontaneity, faith, adventure

    Meaning: Represents new beginnings, a leap of faith, and embracing the unknown. The Fool encourages taking risks and trusting in the journey ahead.

    Astrological Explanation: Uranus represents sudden change, innovation, and the breaking of conventions, aligning with the Fool's themes of new beginnings and unpredictability. The Air element symbolizes intellect, communication, and freedom, reflecting the Fool's free-spirited nature.

  • The Magician Mercury

    Astrological Association: Mercury

    Keywords: Skill, concentration, action, resourcefulness

    Meaning: The Magician represents skill, concentration, and the power to make things happen. It signifies the ability to use one's talents and resources to manifest goals.

    Astrological Explanation: Mercury, the planet of communication and intellect, aligns with the Magician's themes of skill, resourcefulness, and action.

  • The High Priestess Moon

    Astrological Association: Moon

    Keywords: Intuition, mystery, wisdom, subconscious

    Meaning: The High Priestess represents intuition, mystery, and inner wisdom. It signifies the importance of listening to one's inner voice and accessing subconscious knowledge.

    Astrological Explanation: The Moon, associated with intuition, emotions, and the subconscious, aligns with the High Priestess's themes of inner wisdom and mystery.

  • The Emperor Aries (ruled by Mars)

    Astrological Association: Aries (ruled by Mars)

    Keywords: Authority, structure, control, stability

    Meaning: The Emperor embodies authority, structure, and control. He signifies leadership and the need for stability and order in one's life.

    Astrological Explanation: Aries, ruled by Mars, is a sign of leadership, initiative, and assertiveness, which align with the Emperor's qualities of authority and control.

  • The Hierophant Taurus (ruled by Venus)

    Astrological Association: Taurus (ruled by Venus)

    Keywords: Tradition, conformity, morality, ethics

    Meaning: The Hierophant represents tradition, spirituality, and moral values. It signifies the importance of following established conventions and seeking guidance from spiritual leaders.

    Astrological Explanation: Taurus is associated with stability, tradition, and values, while Venus's influence brings a sense of harmony and ethics, mirroring the Hierophant's role as a spiritual and moral guide.

  • The Lovers Gemini (ruled by Mercury)

    Astrological Association: Gemini (ruled by Mercury)

    Keywords: Love, union, relationships, choices

    Meaning: The Lovers symbolize love, harmony, and partnerships. They also represent important choices and decisions, often related to relationships and personal values.

    Astrological Explanation: Gemini, ruled by Mercury, governs communication, duality, and choices, aligning with the Lovers' themes of relationships and decision-making.

  • The Chariot Cancer (ruled by Moon)

    Astrological Association: Cancer (ruled by Moon)

    Keywords: Determination, willpower, control, victory

    Meaning: The Chariot signifies determination, focus, and overcoming obstacles. It represents the triumph of willpower and the ability to steer one's life direction.

    Astrological Explanation: Cancer, ruled by the Moon, is associated with emotions, home, and inner strength, reflecting the Chariot's focus on determination and control over one's path.

  • Strength Leo (ruled by Sun)

    Astrological Association: Leo (ruled by Sun)

    Keywords: Courage, compassion, inner strength, resilience

    Meaning: Strength represents courage, compassion, and resilience. It highlights the importance of inner strength and gentle control over one's passions and instincts.

    Astrological Explanation: Leo, ruled by the Sun, signifies courage, vitality, and leadership, perfectly aligning with the Strength card's themes of inner strength and resilience.

  • The Hermit Virgo (ruled by Mercury)
